Books and records

Having regard to the size and nature of the company's trading, are there material deficiencies in the company's available books and records?
Does the company appear to have ever kept records sufficient to show and explain its transactions and to disclose with reasonable accuracy its financial position at any given time?
 
How long is the gap between the last available accounts (including management accounts) and the end of trading?
 
Can the change in position between the company's last accounts (including management accounts) and your appointment be explained by the available records?
 
Can all the company's transactions with the director(s) and any associated parties be identified?
